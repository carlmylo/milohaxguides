<Aside type="caution">RPCN Menus (for sending or accepting online invites) **will** cause a softlock. You will need an alternative input method to navigate these menus, such as a <a href="../../main#gamepads" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">[typing keyboard or a gamepad.]</a></Aside>
<Aside>Rock Band 3 does not use audio to note MIDI tracking (like Rocksmith), which is what most MIDI guitar kits use. It uses fret sensing and strum detection for notes.  
Don't expect your MIDI guitar to work unless it was made for Rock Band 3.</Aside>


## SYSEX Notice

<Aside type="caution">**You need a MIDI to USB Interface that has SYSEX support!**</Aside>

## Instructions

To connect your Rock Band 3 Pro Guitar, you will need to connect to the MIDI port on the bottom.  
> <Image src={midimustang} alt="A Rock Band 3 Mustang Pro Guitar showing a 5-DIN MIDI output highlighted in blue with a dotted white outline." />

> <Image src={midisquier} alt="A Rock Band 3 Squier Stratocaster Pro Guitar showing a 5-DIN MIDI output highlighted in blue with a dotted white outline." />  

**For this, you will need a MIDI to USB interface**.
