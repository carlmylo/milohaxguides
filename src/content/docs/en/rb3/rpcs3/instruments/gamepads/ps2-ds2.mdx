* It is strongly recommended to get another adapter instead.
	* Get one <a href="https://shop.retrocultmods.com/products/ps2-adapter-for-guitar-drum-dancepad-and-gamepad-controllers" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">[from RetroCultMods]</a>.

	* Get a <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/1397089947" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">[Blue Indigo]</a>.
* This controller auto-strums like god intended.
* To play Guitar/Bass/Keys/Drums parts on a Controller (Padtar), you will need Rock Band 3 Deluxe.
	- You can also rebind your controller within Deluxe.  
	`Menu > Options > Deluxe Settings > Advanced > Controller > Pad Button Remap`
